Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and proof of insurance.

What is the minimum age to rent a car?

The minimum age is 25. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge.

Can I add an additional driver?

Yes, additional drivers can be added for a fee. They must also meet the age and license requirements.
